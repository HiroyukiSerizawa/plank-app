## Tone notes for translators

- **Brutal simplicity**: the copy deliberately rejects "transform your body in 30 days" hyperbole. Keep that flatness.
- **Imperative voice**: "Hit START. Hold. Beat your best." — keep short imperative clauses where the language allows.
- **Sci-fi metaphors**: "piloting a starship" can be adapted to whatever sci-fi reference resonates locally.
- **Anatomical terms**: keep the muscle list (transverse abdominis etc.) — fitness audiences in every market understand these.
- **No emojis** (Play Console policy + neon aesthetic).
- **Section dividers** (`━━━`) render fine in every script — keep them.
